Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Imautotech ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the WatchParty application ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Google Account Sign-In Data

WatchParty uses Google Sign-In (OAuth 2.0) to let you create an account and join rooms with friends. When you choose to sign in with Google, we request and receive the following information from your Google Account:

  • Basic profile information — your name and profile picture, so friends can recognise you inside a watch room
  • Email address — used as your account identifier and to send session notifications, invitations, and important service updates
  • Google user ID — a unique identifier that allows us to recognise your account on subsequent sign-ins

We access this data only through Google's standard OAuth consent screen, and only the minimum scopes required for sign-in (openid, profile, email). You may decline these scopes at any time on the consent screen, and you can revoke access later from your Google Account settings.

Device & Usage Information

We may collect information about your device and how you interact with the App, including device model, operating system version, app version, session duration, and feature usage patterns. This data helps us improve performance and user experience.

Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect and use your advertising ID, device information, and interaction data to deliver personalized ads. Information obtained from your Google Account through Sign-In is never used to serve or personalise advertisements. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Analytics Data

We use analytics services (including Google Analytics and Firebase Analytics) to understand how users interact with the App. This may include information such as crash reports, performance metrics, screen views, and user engagement data.

Limited Use of Google User Data

WatchParty's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically, we affirm that information received from Google APIs is used only to provide or improve user-facing features of WatchParty that are prominent in the App's user interface. We do not:

  • Sell, rent, or transfer Google user data to third parties for advertising, marketing, or any other unrelated purposes
  • Use Google user data for serving advertisements, including retargeting, personalised, or interest-based ads
  • Allow humans to read Google user data, except (a) with the user's explicit consent for specific messages, (b) for security investigations, to prevent abuse, or to comply with law, or (c) where the data has been aggregated and anonymised for internal operations
  • Use Google user data to train or improve generalised or non-personalised machine-learning or AI models

How to Revoke Google Sign-In Access

You may revoke WatchParty's access to your Google Account at any time:

  1. Open myaccount.google.com/permissions
  2. Locate WatchParty under "Third-party apps with account access"
  3. Select it and choose Remove Access

Revoking access signs you out of WatchParty on all devices. You may also request permanent deletion of the data we hold by using the Contact Us page or emailing imautotech@gmail.com.

Permissions We Require

WatchParty enables you to watch movies and content together with friends in real time. To deliver this experience, the App requires the following permissions:

Camera Access

Used for video calls during watch party sessions, allowing you to see and interact with friends while watching content together. Camera access is only active when you explicitly enable it during a session.

Microphone Access

Used for voice chat during watch party sessions, enabling real-time audio communication with other participants. Microphone access is only active when you explicitly enable it during a session.

Notifications

Used to notify you when a friend starts a watch party, sends you an invitation, or when a scheduled session is about to begin. You can manage notification preferences in your device settings at any time.

Internet Access

Required for streaming content, real-time synchronization between participants, serving advertisements via AdMob, and sending analytics data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and create your WatchParty account via Google Sign-In
  • To display your Google profile name and picture inside watch rooms so friends recognise you
  • To provide and maintain the watch party and real-time streaming functionality
  • To enable video and audio communication between participants
  • To send notifications about invitations, sessions, and service updates to your verified email
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob (never personalised using Google Sign-In data)
  • To analyse usage trends, diagnose technical issues, and improve the App
  • To send crash reports and performance data for troubleshooting

Data Retention
